The purpose of this project was to automate a connection to the OpenWeatherMap API, with a graphical output based on various metrics. How do different weather patterns change the closer you get to the equator?

Matplotlib was used for plotting various aspects of weather vs. latitude. Cloudiness, temperature, wind speed, and humidity were looked at in the analysis. Click & look through to see how conditions change with an overview on the results in each.
